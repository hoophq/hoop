# Part 3
	The Flagship Bet — Adaptive Access Control

---

## Adaptive Access Control
	A contextual layer across all our features — like attributes, but it reacts to the situation.

	- Sits on top of guardrails, masking, access requests, everything
	- Lets customers add contextual controls, not just static rules
	- Access decisions that respond to *who, where, when, and how risky*

---

## The signals it reasons over
	The context that should shape every access decision.

	- User identity
	- Geographic location
	- Time of access
	- Device used
	- Device security posture
	- Resource sensitivity
	- Historical behavior
	- Session risk level

---

## Why it matters
	This is the layer that turns Hoop from rules into judgment.

	- Same policy, smarter enforcement — tightened or relaxed by context
	- The natural home for our AI risk analysis to drive real decisions
	- A category-defining story: access that adapts, in real time

---
